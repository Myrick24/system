## 🔄 Order Workflow

### The Process:
```
NEW ORDER (Orange "PENDING")
    ↓
Tap "Start" button
    ↓
PROCESSING (Purple)
    ↓
For Pickups: Tap "Ready"
For Deliveries: Tap "Complete"
    ↓
READY (Green) - Pickups only
    ↓
Customer arrives
    ↓
Tap "Complete"
    ↓
DELIVERED (Teal)
    ↓
Automatically becomes
    ↓
COMPLETED ✓
```
